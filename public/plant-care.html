Plant Care Guide

Use these tips to keep your plants healthy all year.

Watering

Check the soil before watering. Most plants need water when the top inch of soil feels dry.

Sunlight

Place plants where they receive the right amount of light. Some plants need direct sun, while others prefer shade.

Soil

Use quality soil that drains well. Good soil helps roots stay healthy and prevents overwatering problems.

Repotting

Repot plants when roots become crowded or when water drains too quickly through the pot.

Pest Prevention

Inspect leaves regularly. Remove damaged leaves and treat pests early to protect the plant.

Seasonal Care

Adjust watering and sunlight as the seasons change. Plants may need less water during cooler months.
